Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Expiry Tracker ("the App") respects your privacy.

Information We Collect

The App does not require user registration and does not create user accounts.

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Document names and descriptions entered by you
  • Expiry dates and issue dates
  • Categories created by you
  • Notes entered by you
  • Photos attached to documents
  • Notification preferences

How Your Information Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device using a local database.

We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your personal information.

Your information is not transmitted to us.

Photos and Camera Access

If you choose to attach a photo to a document, the App may request access to your camera or photo library.

Photos remain stored on your device and are not uploaded to any server operated by us.

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications reminding you about upcoming expiration dates.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer your information to third parties.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age.
