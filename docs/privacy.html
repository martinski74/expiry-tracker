Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Expiry Tracker ("the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

Information We Collect

The App does not require user registration and does not create user accounts.

The App stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Document names and descriptions entered by you
  • Expiry dates and issue dates
  • Categories created by you
  • Notes entered by you
  • Photos attached to documents
  • Notification preferences

How Your Information Is Stored

All document data, notes, and photos are stored locally on your device using an isolated local database.

We do not operate servers that receive, store, or process your personal document information. Your document data is never transmitted to us.

Photos and Camera Access

If you choose to attach a photo to a document, the App will request access to your camera or photo library. Photos remain stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to any external server.

Notifications

The App requests permission to send local notifications to remind you about upcoming expiration dates. These notifications are triggered entirely on your device and do not use external push servers.

Third-Party Services and In-App Purchases

The App offers premium features via in-app purchases and subscriptions. To manage subscriptions and validate purchases, we use a third-party service called RevenueCat.

RevenueCat may collect non-identifiable transaction data (such as purchase time, product identifier, and subscription status) associated with an anonymous device token. No personal document data or credit card information is shared with or processed by RevenueCat. You can review their privacy policy directly on their official website.

Data Sharing

Except for the subscription validation framework mentioned above, we do not sell, rent, share, or transfer your information to any third parties.

Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until you manually delete it or uninstall the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.
